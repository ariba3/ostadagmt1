Adult vs. Child

Toddler

A toddler is a child approximately 1 to 3 years old, though definitions vary. The toddler years are a time of great cognitive, emotional and social development.

Kid

The term "child" evokes a sense of innocence, curiosity, and the potential for growth, making it a central focus in discussions about education, well-being, and societal responsibilities.

Adult

An adult is a mature individual who has reached the stage of full physical and mental development, typically characterized by the attainment of legal age, different cultures and jurisdictions.
